Get a list of Trump's possible committee IDs

We want to find which of Trump's possible committees have actually received campaign contributions.
  • In the committees file...
  • Match all lines that have TRUMP somewhere
  • Pipe them into another ack to match the first one-or-more word characters starting from the beginning of each line.
  • (i.e. the committee id)
  • Output just the matches into a new text file, trumpc.txt
ack 'TRUMP' committees.txt |
    ack -o '^\w+' > trumpc.txt
